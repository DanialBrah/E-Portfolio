Report

The first assignment that I have done with my teammate is a research about "Comparative Evaluation of 6 Popular Social Media Websites Using Google Lighthouse Matrix."

Summary:

In this Report, I have gained extensive insights into the performance, accessibility, best practices, SEO, and Progressive Web App (PWA) capabilities of some of the most widely used social media platforms, including YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, FB Messenger, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Snapchat. Utilizing the Google Lighthouse tool, I was able to objectively measure and compare these platforms, enhancing my understanding of how these metrics impact user experience and site optimization. This comparative evaluation deepened my technical knowledge of web performance optimization and highlighted the importance of accessibility and adherence to best practices in web development. To improve the assignment, a broader range of metrics could be incorporated to provide a more comprehensive evaluation.
